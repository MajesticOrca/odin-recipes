Are you always on the go, and don't want to compromise a good dinner for you and your family? Then this cheesy slow cooked pasta meal is for you!

Follow the steps below and while you get done everything you need to do through the day, dinner will be ready to eat!

List of ingredients:

  • Elbow Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cheese Soup

Follow the directions listed below:

  1. Boil macaroni until tender.
  2. Add butter to macaroni until melted. Add spices and 1/2 the cheese.
  3. Whisk evaporated milk and eggs, stir into pasta.
  4. Sprinkle the rest of the cheese over the pasta.
  5. Cook on low for 3 hours.
