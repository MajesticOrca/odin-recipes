If you're tired of toast and cereal, we have the solution for you!

Follow the steps below and you can serve yourself breakfast fit for a king!

List of ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Egg

Follow the directions listed below:

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Pour milk, oil and egg mix until smooth.
  3. On a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan, pour batter to desired size.
  4. Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry.
  5. Flip and cook until brown.
  6. Top with your choice of toppings.
