Lasagna Recipe

  • Home

Here is an easy and tasty recipe you can make in under an hour that will leave your entire family wishing they could have it every night!

Follow the steps below and in no time you will be ready to eat!

List of ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Seasoning
  • Noodles
  • Eggs
  • Water

Follow the directions listed below:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cook and drain ground beef, stir in sauce and simmer.
  3. Combine cheese, eggs and seasonings.
  4. Assemble lasagna, cover lightly with foil.
  5. Bake in over for 45 minutes covered.
  6. Uncover and bake for 10 minutes.
